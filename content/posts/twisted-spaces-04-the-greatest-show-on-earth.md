"Humanitarian crisis, immeasurable devastation, end of humanity…"
We had suddenly become the greatest show on Earth —
broadcast in countless languages, prime time was ours.
A pity, really, that all I had to do to become famous
was lose a leg and an arm —
and oh yes, my dear sons as well.
Tragedy sells. And if the social media circus is a gospel,
then salvation awaits us all —
just not today, or tomorrow.
Ah well. All in good time.

— Shayer
