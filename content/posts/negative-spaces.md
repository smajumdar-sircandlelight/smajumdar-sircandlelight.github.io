  <div class="ns-body">
    <p class="drop">There's a concept in visual art called negative space. It's the area around the subject, the part that isn't the thing itself. And yet, without it, the thing falls apart.</p>

    <p>I've been thinking about that a lot. How the gaps matter as much as what fills them. How we rarely give ourselves room to actually sit with an idea before rushing to the next one. This blog is an attempt to do something about that, at least for me.</p>

    <p>Negative Spaces is where I'll bring the things that don't quite fit anywhere else. Opinions I'm still chewing on. Books, films, ideas, or odd little moments that got under my skin for reasons I can't fully explain yet. Some posts will be polished. Others will be barely that. The point isn't perfection, it's presence.</p>

    <p>Part of this is also just the need for a refuge. Life has a way of being relentlessly loud, and I wanted somewhere that isn't. If something here makes you stop for a second, think a little differently, or just feel slightly less alone in your own strange thoughts, then this whole thing will have been worth it.</p>

    <p><em>Welcome. Pull up a chair. There's no agenda here.</em></p>
